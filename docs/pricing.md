# Pricing

This is concept document about pricing, just a place to consider how we might
monetize the product.

## Subscription

The basic model is a subscription service. We might either have a free tier or a
free trial. I don't which would be best. I need to think about it.

## Potential Free Tier

The free trial would allow access to:

 * Basic document management tools
 * Download or print the documents organized 
 * No uploads at all; user is limited to editing text in the site
 * Cost: $0 per year

## Standard Tier

In addition to the features of the free tier, the standard tier would allow
users to:

 * Upload files (100MB limit)
 * Create links to documents for sharing online
 * Cost: $30 per year

## Premium Tier

In addition to the features of the standard tier, the premium tier would allow
users to:

 * Upload files (1GB limit)
 * Email or SMS notifications to remind users of important dates
 * Calendar integration for important dates
 * Upon payment, users receive a full color binder for storing their documents
 * Secure printing and delivery of documents to the user's address (user can
   select which documents to print and have delivered)
 * User can have reprints sent once per year for free, and additional reprints
   for a fee
 * Cost: $100 per year

## Binder One-Off

Users can also purchase a physical binder for storing their documents. This
binder would be a high-quality, durable binder with dividers and pockets for
organizing documents. The binder would be shipped to the user's address.

 * Cost: $30 per binder

## Secure Print One-Off

Users can also purchase a secure printing and delivery service for their
documents. This service would allow users to select which documents they want
printed and delivered to their address. The documents would be printed on high-quality 
paper and securely packaged for delivery.

 * Cost: $20 per order (up to 10 documents, additional documents for a fee)
