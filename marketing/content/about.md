## Why Bob's Binder?

Nobody likes to think about it, but having your affairs in order is one of the most caring things you can do for the people you love.

Bob's Binder was created because too many families are left scrambling to find important documents during the most difficult times of their lives. We believe that getting organized shouldn't be complicated, scary, or expensive.

## How It Works

1. **Create your binder** — Sign up and start adding documents at your own pace.
2. **Follow the checklist** — Our guided checklist walks you through everything from wills to passwords.
3. **Share when ready** — Generate a secure link and unlock code so your trusted person can access everything when the time comes.

## Built with Care

Bob's Binder is designed to be respectful and straightforward. No dark patterns, no pressure — just a helpful tool that lets you take care of business so your family doesn't have to figure it all out on their own.
